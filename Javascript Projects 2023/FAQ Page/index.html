Xbox Games FAQ

My game is an Xbox Play Anywhere title what does that mean

When you purchase an Xbox Play Anywhere title digitally, it's yours to play on both Xbox and Windows. You only need to purchase the game once via the Microsoft Store on your Xbox or Windows device and it'll appear in your game library wherever you sign in. If you've purchased the game on Xbox and want to play it on your Windows device, sign in on your device. If the game is already installed on your device, it'll appear in your list of apps. Otherwise, you can install it by opening the Store app and selecting My library. If you've purchased the game on Windows and want to play on your Xbox console, sign in on your console, press the Xbox button  to open the guide, and select My games & apps > See all > Games. If the game is already installed, it'll appear in your game list. If it's not yet installed, you'll see a download icon on its game tile. Select the game tile and then select Install, or to see more options, select Choose what to install. To learn more about Xbox Play Anywhere and see which games are supported, see: Xbox Play Anywhere


Can I play my game with people on other platforms

Some games support cross play between players on an Xbox console and players on a Windows device for multiplayer mode.


How do i play my game with xbox game pass

If you have a Game Pass subscription, your game is one of the many games included. To install on your Xbox console: Press the Xbox button  to open the guide, and then select My games & apps > See all > Games. Scroll down to Game Pass, and then search for the game tile. Select the tile, then select Install. To install on Windows: Open the Microsoft Store app and search for your game On the product detail page, select Install. If you decide to cancel your Game Pass subscription, you'll no longer be able to launch the game on your Xbox console or Windows devices. You can purchase the game at any time by opening the product detail page and selecting Buy. You won't need to uninstall or reinstall the game if you purchase it after your subscription has ended.
